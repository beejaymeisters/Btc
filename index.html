Claude's call gives a straight yes or no only when the outcome is effectively locked, otherwise a best guess tagged sit out. The scoreboard grades those calls against how Kalshi settled, so the truth shows up over time, not a feeling. Gap speed shows how fast the price is moving, but speed is not direction. Price is blended across Coinbase, Kraken and Bitstamp. This is information, not trading advice, and these moves are close to random, so only risk what you can lose.
