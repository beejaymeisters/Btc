What the words mean
Yes or No, in green or red, is the strongest read. The tool sees real value on that side. Keep size small, Kalshi usually knows more. Best guess Yes or No means only a small edge, not enough to act on, so it says sit out. Sit out with a side, like Yes near certain or No favored, shows which way it leans while there is no money left in it, either already priced in or priced fairly. Coin toss means about fifty fifty, nothing to lean on. Sit out, too unstable means a pump or the exchanges disagree, so the read cannot be trusted. Warming up means it is still gathering price and market data.
